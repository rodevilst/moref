GET IN TOUCH

Earn money with us

You can sell your film to us, and we can buy non-exclusive rights to your film and get you a percentage of the non-exclusive direct sales and/or monetisation we make.

ULTRA V acquires only non-exclusive film rights. This way, authors are free to make changes to the film, resell it and broadcast it onto other channels and generally continue to use it as they please, while we keep paying you the share of profits.

We are interested in films of any year of production — short films, feature films, both animation and live-action, as well as documentaries.

Payment for the film is calculated per minute, starting from $5 — $9. The cost gets higher depending on the type, subject, length of your film, language of the dialogues, master-files available; poster, photos, and documents availability, etc. The cost of full-length feature or documentary films is much higher.

ABOUT ULTRA V and the team:

ULTRA V (UKRKINO) film company has been distributing movies to festivals as well as to specialized TV-channels and online-platforms since 2014. Our films were successful at over 30 festivals all over the world, including Rotterdam IFF, Sundance IFF, Tallinn Black Nights IFF, Warsaw IFF and others. The database we work with includes over 200 festivals.

We cooperate with many Ukrainian and international partners, such as Shorts TV, Amazon Prime, etc. We have positive experience selling films to such Ukrainian TV channels and VOD platforms as 1+1, TET, Megogo, OLL.tv, Kyivstar.tv, etc.
